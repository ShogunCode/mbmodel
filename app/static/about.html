Getting Started

Accessing the Application:

Visit our homepage and register for an account if you haven't done so. Login with your credentials to access the application.

Navigating the Interface:

Our application features a clean, modern interface designed for straightforward navigation. You will find the main functions in the top navigation bar which include Home, Upload Data, View Results, and Help.


Uploading Data

Preparing Your Data:

Ensure your data is in the correct format as specified in our guidelines. Typically, this includes structured files such as .csv or .txt formats containing tumor sample information.

Uploading Your Dataset:

Click on 'Upload Data' in the navigation menu. Select your prepared data file from your device and confirm the upload. Once uploaded, our algorithms will start processing your data. This process may take some time depending on the size and complexity of the dataset.


Viewing and Understanding Results

Accessing Results:

Once your data has been processed, navigate to the 'View Results' page via the top menu. Here, you will see a dashboard displaying the classification results in both graphical and tabular formats.

Interpreting Graphs and Tables:

Graphs: Visual representations of the classification will show how tumor samples distribute across the seven subgroups. Tables: Detailed tabular data provides subgroup classifications along with probability scores and other relevant metrics.

Exporting Results:

You can download the results in various formats for further analysis or presentation purposes by clicking the 'Download' button available on the results page.


Updating Data and Continuous Learning

Our model is periodically updated with new training data to improve accuracy and adapt to the latest research findings. Users are encouraged to submit their validated data to contribute to these updates.
